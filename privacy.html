Privacy Policy

At File Tools Suite, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

File Processing

Most tools run entirely on your device, meaning your files never leave your browser. If server-side processing is needed for heavy conversions, files are stored temporarily and deleted automatically.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information. Any data entered in forms (such as contact messages) is only used for support purposes.

Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies for analytics and improving user experience. Third-party advertisers like Google AdSense may also place cookies for ad personalization.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of your data by contacting us at support@filetoolsuite.com.
