    Guides & FAQs

    How to compress PDFs well?

    Client‑side re‑saving gives small gains. For real size cuts, use server tools like Ghostscript/ImageMagick. We provide best‑practice settings and examples. Avoid scanning at too high DPI, prefer grayscale when possible, and compress images before embedding.
